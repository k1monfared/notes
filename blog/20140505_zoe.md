# Zoe

[![Zoe](files/20140505/2014-05-03-19-52-18.jpg)](http://www.zoekeating.com/)

Zoe Keating's concert was amazing. Out of the big concerts that I have gone to during the past few years, hers exactly matched my (very high) expectations, not more, not less. She is constantly working on the old pieces to improve them. Out of all, the new version of the **Path** from the album **Into the trees** was mind blowing. Her new album is coming out in two moths.
