# Why did we?

I have students asking me why did we buy this book, and I've always had a hard time understanding that question, let alone answering it in a convincing way. Until I read the following.
> "This is why “Why did we have to buy this book?” was such a great question. The student who asked it was not complaining. He was trying to understand how the magic worked."
Read the interesting article "[Live and Learn](http://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2011/06/06/live-and-learn-2)", by [Louis Menand](http://www.newyorker.com/contributors/louis-menand).

I still can't answer that question, but I understand it.
