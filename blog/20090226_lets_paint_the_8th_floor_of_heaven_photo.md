# Let's Paint the 8th Floor of Heaven - photo

![Let's Paint the 8th Floor of Heaven](files/photoblog/2009-02-26_lets_paint_the_8th_floor_of_heaven_01.jpg)

![Let's Paint the 8th Floor of Heaven](files/photoblog/2009-02-26_lets_paint_the_8th_floor_of_heaven_02.jpg)

Isfahan, 8-Behesht

It was about the sunset and the worker was painting the canal inside the garden 2 days before begining the new year (Nowrooz). I shoot and the camera flashed. Since I was thinking that I have turned it off I surprised and get the camera down in order to turn the flash off. The painter had realized the light and was looking around in horror.
