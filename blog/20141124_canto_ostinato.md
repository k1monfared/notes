# Canto Ostinato

[embed]https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JDCsOL2vBJc[/embed]

Incredible minimalist music for multiple pianos, by [Simeon Ten Holt](http://www.simeontenholt.com/).

To learn more about this piece read [this](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canto_Ostinato), and there's a documentary made about it: [*Over Canto*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_KL-407H6vQ).
