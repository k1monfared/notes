# 16-Week Olympic Triathlon Training Plan

I put together an interactive training plan for an Olympic-distance triathlon (1500m swim, 40km bike, 10km run). It's a 16-week progressive plan with 6 workouts per week (2 swims, 2 bikes, 2 runs) and recovery weeks built in at weeks 4, 8, and 12.

The plan is a single self-contained HTML page with some nice features:

- Pick your rest day and start date
- Export the entire plan to your calendar (.ics file)
- Download as PDF
- Toggle between color and black & white for printing
- Includes an intensity guide and terminology reference

Check it out here: [16-Week Olympic Triathlon Training Plan](https://k1monfared.github.io/triathlon-training-plan/)

Source code on [GitHub](https://github.com/k1monfared/triathlon-training-plan).
